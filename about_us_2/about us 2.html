FAQs

What is TrustNet Health+? expand_more

TrustNet Health+ is a mobile app that connects you with hospital dispatchers during emergencies for faster response times.

How do I use the SOS feature? expand_more

Simply tap the large SOS button on the main screen. This will immediately send your location and medical information to the nearest hospital dispatcher.

Is my personal data secure? expand_more

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your information is only shared with medical professionals during an active emergency.
