Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile?

expand_more

To update your profile, go to the 'Profile' tab, tap on the 'Edit Profile' button, make your desired changes, and then tap 'Save'.

Is my personal data secure?

expand_more

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your personal data is protected at all times. Your privacy is our top priority.

How can I reset my password?

expand_more

On the login screen, tap the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you instructions to reset your password.
