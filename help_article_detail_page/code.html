How do I update my profile information?

Keeping your profile information up to date is essential for a seamless healthcare experience. This guide will walk you through the process step by step.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Navigate to the 'Profile' section from the main menu.

2. Tap on the 'Edit Profile' button located at the top right corner of your screen.

3. Update your personal details, contact information, or insurance provider as needed.

4. Tap 'Save Changes' to confirm your updates. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
